# 002 — User Personas

## Persona 1: The Activity + Remote Worker

**Name**: Marco, 31, software engineer
**Stay type**: Surf + remote work, 3 weeks

**Goals**
- Surf 2–3 hours every morning before work
- Have 5–6 focused work hours daily
- Not lose productive momentum during the trip

**Constraints**
- Needs reliable wifi (video calls, GitHub)
- Needs a proper work spot (not just hotel wifi)
- Budget: mid-range ($60–120/night)

**What they optimize for**
- Proximity of surf break to work spot (ideally walkable)
- Work infrastructure quality over beach views
- Predictable daily rhythm

**What makes a recommendation fail**
- Work spot is far from surf (different micro-area)
- Wifi quality is unknown or unverified
- No morning coffee before work option
- Recommendation based on vibes, not evidence

## Persona 2: The Deep Worker

**Name**: Sofia, 35, startup founder
**Stay type**: Quiet city or mountain base, 4 weeks

**Goals**
- 8–10 focused work hours daily
- Minimal daily friction
- Good gym nearby

**Constraints**
- Strong internet is non-negotiable
- Calls need quiet, stable environment
- No party areas
- Budget: less of a concern

**What they optimize for**
- Work environment quality above everything
- Low noise, predictable schedule
- Gym within 15 min walk

**What makes a recommendation fail**
- Area has nightlife noise
- Coworking is far or requires booking
- Gym not nearby
- No food walkability

## Persona 3: The Routine-First Traveler

**Name**: Lena, 28, designer
**Stay type**: Any activity, 2 weeks

**Goals**
- Maintain their home-city routine as closely as possible
- Gym, good coffee, predictable food

**Constraints**
- Must have gym within walking distance
- Must have laptop-friendly cafe within 10 min
- Grocery within reasonable distance

**What they optimize for**
- Daily friction reduction
- Routine continuity over novelty
- Walkability score

**What makes a recommendation fail**
- No gym option near the base
- Unpredictable food or grocery access
- Requires scooter for daily essentials
- Recommendation treats transport as easy without checking

## Persona 4: The Budget Explorer

**Name**: Alex, 25, freelancer
**Stay type**: Any activity, 3–6 weeks

**Goals**
- Find the most functional base for the lowest cost
- Make it work with cheap options

**Constraints**
- Budget: $30–60/night total (accommodation + food)
- May not have scooter
- Willing to compromise on comfort

**What they optimize for**
- Budget value score
- Free or low-cost work options
- Cheap but reliable food nearby

**What makes a recommendation fail**
- Recommendation assumes car/scooter
- "Budget" area actually requires higher spend for daily life
- No cheap food within walking distance

---

## Persona 5: The Premium Comfort Seeker

**Name**: Rachel, 40, senior consultant
**Stay type**: Relaxation + work maintenance, 2 weeks

**Goals**
- Beautiful comfortable accommodation
- Good food and coffee
- Light work maintenance (4h/day)

**Constraints**
- Budget: not a concern
- Needs quiet, comfortable environment
- Won't compromise on sleep quality

**What they optimize for**
- Sleep + comfort score
- Food and coffee quality
- Vibe match
- Activity access is secondary

**What makes a recommendation fail**
- Noisy area despite premium price
- Work infra not trustworthy
- No quality restaurant option nearby
- "Premium" area has poor wifi
